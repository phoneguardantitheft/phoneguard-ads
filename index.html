Features

Designed to help you know when someone touched your device.

Trap Mode

Shows a decoy lock screen and captures evidence after incorrect attempts.

Motion Alert

Plays a loud alarm when your phone is picked up or moved.

Evidence Vault

Store captured evidence with protected access to prevent deletion.

Bluetooth Tracker Scan

Detect unknown Bluetooth devices near you (like unwanted trackers).

Simple Setup

Quick configuration so you can enable protection in seconds.

Built for Privacy

No tracking. Evidence stays on-device unless you choose to share it.

Privacy

PhoneGuard is designed with privacy in mind.

  • No user tracking. The app does not track you across other companies’ apps or websites.
  • Local-first evidence. Evidence is stored on your device.
  • User control. You decide if/when anything is shared.
